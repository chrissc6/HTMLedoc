Programming

How to start?

  1. Pick a language:
    • Any language it doesnt matter that much... at first.
    • The goal here is to start.
    • Pick python if you cant decide.
    • C# is my personal favorite.

  2. Initiate research:
    • Youtube, Google, ect.
    • See if you enjoy it.

  3. Complete "Hello World":
    • Refer to step 2.

  4. Study the basics:
    • Data types.
    • Variables.
    • Constructs.
    • Data structure.

  5. Make a bigger program:
    • Refer to step 2.

  6. Study the basics part 2:
    • Low vs High level languages.
    • Compile vs Interpret.
    • Paradigms.
    • History.

  7. Make a bigger program:
    • Refer to step 2.

Tips:

When you begin your journey into programming, consider that you're learning two things at the same time.

  1. Language syntax:
    • How to write/ format your code.
  2. Problem solving:
    • How to structure your instructions together.
Make something that excites you.
